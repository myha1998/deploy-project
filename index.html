Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What areas do you service?

We cover Northern Virginia (Fairfax, Arlington, Prince William, Loudoun), Washington DC, and select Maryland suburbs (Montgomery & Prince George's counties).

Do your packages include chemicals?

Yes! All our maintenance packages (Weekly & Bi-Weekly) include chemicals as part of the service.

How long does a pool opening take?

Typically 1-2 days depending on the pool size and condition. Our team handles everything from cover removal to chemical balancing.

Do you offer lifeguard staffing for commercial pools?

Absolutely. We provide full turnkey lifeguard management — recruitment, training, scheduling, and supervision — for apartment complexes, HOAs, and hotels.

Can I schedule a one-time service visit?

Yes. Additional visits are available at $140 per visit for existing customers. One-time services for new customers are quoted based on scope.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are fully licensed in VA, DC, and MD, and carry comprehensive liability insurance. We are also BBB accredited and members of PHTA.

What's the difference between Weekly and Bi-Weekly maintenance?

Weekly includes 15 visits per season with more frequent chemical monitoring. Bi-Weekly includes 8 visits. Both include opening and closing.

Do you handle commercial health code inspections?

Yes. We assist with health department compliance and can represent property owners during inspections in all three jurisdictions.
